About

I am a California licensed esthetician. I received my education at Paul Mitchell the School San Jose, in 2015. I’ve always had a passion for the beauty industry watching tons of YouTube videos, buying different products and even working at Sephora as a studio coordinator for about 2 years.
Fast forward to May 2018, I gave birth to a spunky little girl who gave me the encouragement and push I needed to open my own business. Although I was terrified of being a new mom and a business owner it was something I wanted for so long.
Two months after my daughter was born I opened “Waxed By Dra” on July 18, 2018. I’ve made great friends amongst my many clients and have the opportunity to introduce myself to many more, I would not be where I am now without any of them.
I am forever grateful for the continued support and referrals I get daily and only hope to keep growing my business to suit everyone’s needs.

- Draci Gomez
